JomInvois Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

JomInvois ("we", "our", "the app") is developed by Tripex Support Art. This policy explains how your data is handled when you use the JomInvois mobile application.

1. Data We Collect

JomInvois stores all invoice, business, and customer data locally on your device using SQLite. We do not transmit this data to any server owned by us.

The app connects to the following third-party services on your behalf:

  • LHDN MyInvois API — to submit e-Invoice documents as required by Malaysian law. Data transmitted includes invoice details, your business registration number, and your TIN as provided by you.
  • Google Drive — if you choose to enable backup, your invoice database is uploaded directly to your own Google Drive account. We never have access to this data.
  • Google AdMob — if you are on the free tier, anonymised advertising identifiers may be used to serve ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing — if you subscribe to JomInvois Premium, payment is processed by Google Play. We do not store payment information.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any personally identifying information
  • Invoice or customer data to our own servers
  • Location data
  • Device contacts or files outside of the app's storage

3. Remote Configuration

The app may fetch a configuration file from a public GitHub repository to enable emergency service updates (e.g., if the LHDN API changes). This request contains no personal data — it is a standard public URL fetch.

4. Crash Reporting

The app does not include crash reporting or analytics SDKs. No telemetry is collected.

5. Children's Privacy

JomInvois is a business tool intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can delete the app to remove all local data. For Google Drive backups, you can delete the "JomInvois Backups" folder in your own Google Drive.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
